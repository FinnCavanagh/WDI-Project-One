Danger connect 4! Basic 2 players Player 1 is red, player 2 is yellow The grid is X x X big Player 1 goes first to choose which column to drop a piece into The piece gets dropped Player 2 chooses which column to drop the second piece into The piece gets dropped This continues until one player gets X pieces in a row, in a column, row or diagonally. When a player achieves this, the pieces will explode and the game / grid crumbles. Potential Extras The game will be on a timer and as the time is nearly up parts of the grid / game will start to crumble away. Just as a player is about to get 4 in a row they'll be asked a question - if get get it wrong one of their pieces disappear.
